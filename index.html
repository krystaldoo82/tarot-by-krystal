Tarot by Krystal

Medium and Tarot Readings

Krystal is a small town woman just trying to make sense of the world. She has always had a talent for reading tarot cards and has been doing so for over 18 years, using the same deck all this time. She is intuitive and honest, and passes no judgement when it comes to lifes ups and downs. She understands that sometimes we make bad decisions but that doesn't make us a bad person.

Not too long ago she learned she has a knack for knowing things about people from their photos. She was given photos of those that had passed and she intuitively just "knew" things about them and sometimes could get messages from them. She never knows how much information she will get.
